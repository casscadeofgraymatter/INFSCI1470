## Overview

**Idlecast** is a slow paced, cozy idle fishing game where your tiny island gradually grows into a peaceful oasis. Cast your line, collect coins, discover new species of fish, and upgrade your surroundings, all without lifting a finger.

Whether you leave it running in the background while studying or sit and watch the water ripple, Idlecast keeps ticking along at its own gentle pace. It's simple and relaxing, designed to be a calm corner of your screen.

Inspired by **Shar's Frog Pond** and **Moczan's Ropuka's Idle Island**.

---

## Sections

The game is built around a single evolving scene, with several interconnected systems the player manages over time.

| System | Description |
|---|---|
| 🎣 **Fishing** | Your rod casts and reels automatically, earning coins with every catch |
| 🐟 **Fish Discovery** | New species unlock as your pond population grows |
| 🏝️ **Island Upgrades** | Spend coins to add new decorations and structures to your scene |
| 🦞 **Companion NPC** | Jebediah the lobster guides you with contextual tips and hints |

---

## Features

### Automatic Fishing
Your character fishes on their own. Every catch earns coins and contributes to your fish discovery log.

### Fish Discovery System
The pond starts sparse, but as you invest coins into restocking your waters, new and rarer species begin to appear.

### Jebediah
Your lobster companion surfaces at key moments to nudge you toward your next action, like restocking the pond or spending your first coins.

### Island Upgrades
Coins unlock cosmetic and environmental upgrades, gradually transforming your bare island into a cozy, lived-in scene.

## Structure

```
Idlecast/
├── Idlecast.exe              # Main executable
├── Idlecast_Data/            # Unity build data
│   ├── Managed/              # Game assemblies
│   ├── Resources/            # Assets and scene data
│   └── StreamingAssets/      # Audio and additional resources
└── UnityCrashHandler64.exe   # Unity crash handler
```

## Built With

- **Unity** — Game engine and scene management
- **C#** — All gameplay logic, fishing systems, and UI
- **Blender** — Low poly 3D assets and environment modeling

## Installation

1. Head to [cxsserole.itch.io/idlecast](https://cxsserole.itch.io/idlecast)
2. Click **Download** and grab `Idlecast.zip` (~87 MB)
3. Extract the zip to a folder of your choice
4. Run `Idlecast.exe`, no installation required

> **Note:** Windows may show a SmartScreen warning since the game is unsigned. Click "More info, Run anyway" to proceed. The game is safe!
